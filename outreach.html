Community & Outreach

We believe in giving back. From local STEM workshops to mentoring junior FLL teams, we are committed to spreading our passion for robotics.

STEM Workshops

We host weekend workshops at Harmony School of Innovation where elementary students can learn the basics of block coding and mechanical design using LEGO sets.


Impact: Over 200 hours of volunteer service logged in 2024.

Mentorship

Our team members dedicate time to mentor local FIRST LEGO League (FLL) teams, guiding them through their project research and robot game strategies.


Collaboration: We partner with 3 local middle schools to support their after-school robotics programs.
